2019 Tournament Schedule

Good Evening Families!

The Skiles Test baseball and softball league tournament will be held between June 3rd to June 15th, 2019. This is the updated version of the tournament schedule, and it's important to check your league for changes to times and dates. The posted schedule on the website has been updated to reflect the changes as well. You may view it here. You will find a list of tournament game makeup dates below, should there be any weather related cancellations.

Baseball divisions will follow a double elimination schedule, while softball will play single elimination.

Tournament Rule Changes

Below are important rule changes applied during tournament of which to take note.

  1. All the normal rules apply.
  2. There are no ties during tournament.
  3. If there is a tie at the time limit, a new inning will begin.
  4. A game stopped due to weather will be resumed at a time determined by the League Commissioner.
