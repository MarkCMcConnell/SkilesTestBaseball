Pre-Season Evaluations

Our pre-season evaluations is the first major event of the Skiles Test season.  The last couple years we have setup a system to nearly eliminate lines and delays.  We will need volunteers and coaches to help evaluate the players in preparation for the player draft.  If you are interested in helping, please reach out to: enrollment@skilestest.com

Evaluations will be held on Sunday February 25th at Lawrence Central High School.  You need to bring your players sometime between 11a and 4p - it takes around 20 to 30 minutes to complete the activities.
