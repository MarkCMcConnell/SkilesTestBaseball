Grounds and Safety

Skiles Test Elementary is under current renovation and that activity spreads into the parking lots and the grounds of Skiles Test Youth Baseball & Softball.  While you are at Skiles Test please keep everyone close by and don't allow anyone in the construction areas, this includes the location of the two fields being built.  Now that the weather is improving we hope that the fields are build in a timely manner, but they are not a play area until after their completion.  The area is already marked for construction to start and any activity in the area can disrupt the progress.  This past weekend marking stakes for the new fields were pulled up which will require work to be redone.  The area is accessible by the public, so we don't believe the damaged was caused by Skiles Test patrons, but this is a good reminder to make sure we set a good example.

While attending games always be aware of the activity on the fields.  Baseballs and softballs tend to fly off the field in foul territory.  Make sure your family is close and aware of the risks.  

Prior to baseball and softball games our fields do require preparation and markings.  If you are interested in helping prior to your player's games we have some helpful instructions on our websites.  Our coaches willsurley appreciate any help to allow them time to prepare their team to play ball.  Instructions are available here: /Site/Fields-Weather/Facilities/Field-Preparation
