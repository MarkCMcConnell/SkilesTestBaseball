Good afternoon Skiles Test Families,

Thank you for everyone that came out for our preseason player evaluations.  It was great to see everyone and start to discuss the upcoming season!  Thank you to the coaches and volunteers that spent their day working and all the families that brought their player to be evaluated.  Based on the responses we are already working on improvements for next season.  If you have any thoughts or ideas, please share them with operations@skilestest.com.

The number of teams per division will be set this week and we will only allow players to register to fill out the remaining spots to even out numbers between teams.  Parents will be notified about team assignments in mid-March.
