Fall Baseball Clinic Series 

Skiles Test is launching an inaugural Fall Series of Clinics to evaluate interest in future Fall season activities.  Available to all ages and genders the clinics will focus on baseball skills over a 6 week set of sessions each Sunday from 4:30pm - 7:30pm in September into October, each including an instructional session and a scrimmage.  This schedule should allow you to participate in other fall sports and activities and still attend the clinics.  

The first session is Sunday September 9th at 4:30 PM at Skiles Test Elementary.  

Players and their parents are encouraged to attend as there will be a focus on drills that can be run at home to further improve your players skills.  

If you have any questions, please contact enrollment@skilestest.com.  

Registration is now open, login to you SkilesTest.com account to sign up.  The costs is $60 total for participation in all six sessions and will include an official league shirt.  Participants are encouraged to sign up in advance, but registration will be available throughout the series.  Walk up registration is welcome, but players must be registered online; you can register on-site online and show the organizers your emailed receipt.
