** Upgrade your involvement **

It takes a lot of help to run our league each season.  Whether you are a parent, grandparent, alumni, coach, committee member, or if you just have time to lend a hand; let one of our board members know you are interested.  There is no need to be a baseball and softball expert to get involved, we have plenty of tasks that cover all different areas.  Our teams are divided into a few different groups:  Concessions, Enrollment, Finance, Grounds, Operations, and Promotions.  Contact our league president, Tony Rondinella email: president@skilestest.com
