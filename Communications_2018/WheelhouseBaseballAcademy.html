Wheelhouse Baseball Academy

A new baseball academy has opened in our neighborhood and they are extended discounts and incentives to participants from our league.  Wheelhouse Baseball Academy is located on 65th Street just off Binford Blvd.  If you have a group of 3-5 players or are interested in your player joining a group from Skiles Test, contact promotions@skilestest.com to express interest.  The academy also provides opportunities for private and group instruction for hitting, pitching, and more. 

Check our website for additional programs and opportunities!  We will add more as we hear about them.
