** Upgrade your involvement **

It takes a lot of help to run our league each season.  Whether you are a parent, grandparent, alumni, coach, committee member, or if you just have time to lend a hand; let one of our board members know you are interested.  There is no need to be a baseball and softball expert to get involved, we have plenty of tasks that cover all different areas.  Our teams are divided into a few different groups:  Concessions, Enrollment, Finance, Grounds, Operations, and Promotions.  Contact our league president, Tony Rondinella email: president@skilestest.com

A new season is almost upon us

It is not too early to start planning for next season!  Our 2018 Spring Recreational Baseball and Softball seasons will start registering in early January.  Similar to past years we will host our player evaluations in mid-February, start practices in mid-March and get into our full season in early April.  If you have any questions, please contact: enrollment@skilestest.com.

Wheelhouse Baseball Academy

A new baseball academy has opened in our neighborhood and they are extended discounts and incentives to participants from our league.  Wheelhouse Baseball Academy is located on 65th Street just off Binford Blvd.  If you have a group of 3-5 players or are interested in your player joining a group from Skiles Test, contact promotions@skilestest.com to express interest.  The academy also provides opportunities for private and group instruction for hitting, pitching, and more.
