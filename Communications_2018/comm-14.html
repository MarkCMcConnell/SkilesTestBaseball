Umpires - Put your Middle School and High School Kids to Work!

Skiles Test employees (yes, pays!) youth umpires to officiate games.  Professional umpires are also used in games, but we have a need for a lot of kids to work during the season.  Games are scheduled most week nights and Saturdays.  We schedule about a week in advance and we work around other schedules and conflicts.  This is a great opportunity to get your older kids out of the house and start making there own money to spend.  We have a great system to bring in inexperienced kids and turn them into confident umpires.

If you are interested reach out to operations@skilestest.com
