Team assignments were released over the weekend.  If your coach has not reached out to you yet, they should be in contact with you soon.  Below is you current team roster including selected uniform sizes and team sponsor. 

Please carefully review your player's uniform size and notify us immediately if a change is required.  The size listed should be based on the selections made at registration adjusted with any changes made at the evaluation event.  This is your last opportunity to adjust the size you will receive.  Based on the amount of time and personalization that is included in our uniforms, changes are not available later in the season.

If a change is required for your player, please contact enrollment@skilestest.com as soon as possible and ensure that we respond and confirm your change.
