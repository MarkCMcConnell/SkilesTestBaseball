Welcome New Families!

We have had a good number of new families joining us this year, and we are very glad you have chosen to play at Skiles Test for this upcoming season.  We have a staff who is eager to help and a roster of long standing families that make Skiles Test the best league in town.  We have a collection of pages on our web site under the About Us->Player section intended to help new participants get familiar with our league.  There are some details below about our Evaluation Day event which is the first event of the season for all leagues, except Rookies who do not need to attend; we will have people available that day to discuss any questions or concerns you may have.  If you have any additional questions, please reach out to enrollment@skilestest.com.
