2018 Closing Ceremonies

On Saturday June 16th following the last Championship game we will end the season with our Closing Ceremonies.  All teams will be recognized and players will be awarded for the season.  We will present our regular season and tournament champion and runner-up awards.  Have your players wear their uniform shirt (shorts and tennis shoes are fine) and gather with their teams at 2:15PM.  Teams will be introduced following the conclusion of the last game, expected to be the Major League Baseball game.  The Ceremonies will be hosted in the same area at Skiles Test as the Opening Ceremonies.  The presentation should be brief.

 

End of Season Celebration Dinner -- Why not Chile Verde?

Chile Verde Mexican Restaurant & Grill is hosting a fundraiser for Skiles Test this Saturday June 16th.  After Closing Ceremonies head over to Chile Verde, mention "Skiles Test" when you pay your bill and 30% of the proceeds will be given back to Skiles Test Youth Baseball & Softball.

Parking at Skiles Test

Yesterday afternoon we were notified by Lawrence Township Schools that the Skiles Test parking lot was going to be torn up immediately as part of the ongoing school and field construction.  For the remaining games during this week there should be plenty of parking available - cars are able to park in the front lot and the bus pickup area.  The back parking lot - although under repair - should be available throughout the week and the weekend for parking.  It may be in a rough stone covering or fresh asphalt, but it should be uninhibited and available for use.  Street parking is available in the surrounding neighborhoods for overflow.
