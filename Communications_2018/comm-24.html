Next Season... a reason to Celebrate!

Thank you to everyone for accommodating the construction around Skiles Test Elementary this past season.  Next season we expect to be back to normal with all games being played at a single park.  The investment we made at LECC will continue to benefit all players by expanding our field time for practices.  It will be exciting to have the Skiles Test Game Day experience return to our park off Johnson Road. The facility improvements that were available this year with new bathrooms and concession stand has been a nice start.  Before the season starts we will rededicate our two front fields as we start the next era of Skiles Test Youth Baseball & Softball.  

Registration will open for Spring 2019 in December.
