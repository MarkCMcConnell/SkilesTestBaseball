Good Morning Skiles Test Rookie Participants,

We are actively seeking additional coaches for this upcoming Spring season in the division your player participates.  We have already had a great number of registrations and we will have more teams than expected.  Additional coaches will allow us to build more teams each with a fewer number of players, providing more playing opportunity for everyone.  The Rookie division is a great opportunity to start your coaching career.  If you have any interest in coaching or participating, please let us know.  Contact rookie@skilestest.com.  If you are interested in coaching, but have concerns about experience or knowledge, we have a few different opportunities to help you out.

On Saturday February 24th, Skiles Test Youth Baseball & Softball is hosting a coaching clinic in cooperation with the Lawrence Central High School Athletic Department and Bears Baseball.  This event will be a great opportunity to learn the keys to scheduling a successful practice and experience drills that help youth learn the game and improve their skills.

For additional assistance for the coaches that may have troubles committing to being available for every practice, we can pair up multiple individuals who are willing to work cooperatively to coach a team.  If you have interest, contact us and we can help pair you up with other willing individuals.

If you believe you have signed up to coach, but you have not been contacted yet directly by a board member, please contact us.
