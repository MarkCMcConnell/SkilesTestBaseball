Postseason Tournament  **  UPDATED SCHEDULE

Skiles Test concludes each season with postseason tournaments for each division between our league teams.  

For 2018, we will continue with the double elimination bracket format with a single Championship game used last year.  The Rookie Baseball League does not compete in a tournament.  All tournaments begin by June 4th continue throughout the week and the June 11th weekend and will conclude on Saturday June 16th, 2018.  Sunday June 17th will be used as a rain makeup date with slightly later start times.
