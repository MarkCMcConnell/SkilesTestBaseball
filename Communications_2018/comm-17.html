Concessions Help

We are looking for a few big helpers that are willing to help manage our concession stand this season.  We have all new facilities on site at Skiles Test and we need someone who can help with the day to day operations.  We use volunteers to help with the point of sale so a lot of support is available.  This is a big job but very important to be able to provide this service.  With additional games taking place at the LECC facility this season, while field work is completed at Skiles Test, we have an extra need this year to make concessions available.  If you are interested in helping out, please contact: Tony Rondinella, president@skilestest.com

 

Umpires - Put your Middle School and High School Kids to Work!

Skiles Test employees (yes, pays!) youth umpires to officiate games.  Professional umpires are also used in games, but we have a need for a lot of kids to work during the season.  Games are scheduled most week nights and Saturdays.  We schedule about a week in advance and we work around other schedules and conflicts.  This is a great opportunity to get your older kids out of the house and start making there own money to spend.  We have a great system to bring in inexperienced kids and turn them into confident umpires.

If you are interested reach out to operations@skilestest.com
