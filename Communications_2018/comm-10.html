Pre-Season Player Evaluations 

Evaluations are Sunday February 25th at Lawrence Central High School.  You must bring your players sometime between 11a and 3p; expect it to take around 20 to 30 minutes to complete the activities.  The last couple years we have setup a system to nearly eliminate lines and delays.  Player registration will be available on-site, but have your player registered by Thursday 5:00pm to shorten the time to complete evaluations.  Everyone who has registered online and paid their deferred balance or requested a payment arrangement can skip "registration" and proceed straight to "Express Check-In" next Sunday.

* Rookie League players do not evaluate.  You will be contacted about team assignments in March.

Programs like 'Bring-A-Friend' require all players to evaluate to maintain even teams.

We need additional Coaches

Coaching is a great experience to share with your kids.  Teach a new generation of players the skills to America's Pastime.  We seeking coaches for every division.  If you are interested in coaching, please contact our Director of Baseball Operations, Brian Queck: baseball@skilestest.com or Director of Softball Operations, Eric Burton: eric@skilestest.com

All Coaches for all divisions must register to coach in our league.  If you intend to coach and have not registered - login to your account, find your name, and select Register to complete the form for Head or Assistant coaching.  Coach registration must be complete to participate in the player draft - scheduled for March 1st.

Umpires - Put your Middle School and High School Kids to Work!

Skiles Test employees (yes, pays!) youth umpires to officiate games.  Professional umpires are also used in games, but we have a need for a lot of kids to work during the season.  Games are scheduled most week nights and Saturdays.  We schedule about a week in advance and we work around other schedules and conflicts.  This is a great opportunity to get your older kids out of the house and start making there own money to spend.  We have a great system to bring in inexperienced kids and turn them into confident umpires.

If you are interested reach out to operations@skilestest.com
